Executive Summary

"Project Sentry" solves a specific national vulnerability: the lack of skilled defenders capable of protecting cloud-native critical infrastructure. Unlike general IT programs, this curriculum bypasses entry-level support skills to focus exclusively on Data Sovereignty, Cloud Segmentation, and AI Counter-Adversarial Operations.

Core Modules

Advanced training for the next generation of federal defenders.

MOD 01

The Doctrine of Data Segregation

Preventing "Cascading Failures" in National Infrastructure through rigorous cloud architecture.

  • The "Vault" Theory (VPC Design)
  • Government Data Sovereignty (FedRAMP)
  • IAM as a Weapon (Least Privilege)
MOD 02

AI as the Shield & Sword

Defending against the next generation of automated attacks using MITRE ATLAS.

  • Mapping Adversarial AI Threats
  • Model Poisoning Defense Pipelines
  • Fighting AI with Local Agents
MOD 03

The LLM Defense Grid

Securing the Government's use of Generative AI against Prompt Injection.

  • Team Bravo: Power Grid (SCADA)
  • Team Charlie: Gov (Voter Database)
